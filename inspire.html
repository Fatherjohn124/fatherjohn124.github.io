What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Aj Brown is currently a NFL wide receiver who currently plays for the Philadelphia Eagles and formerly played for the Tennessee Titans. Aj Brown is a very impactful player and has made a name for himself on and off the field recently opening up about his mental health stuggles and becoming a advocate for others that are struggling with mental health problems as well.
